Sandalwood Beard Oil

 

Our premium Sandalwood Beard Oil helps hydrate, condition and add shine to your client's beard while moisturizing the skin beneath it. Our cold-pressed, eight oil blend, helps to prevent itchiness and flaking that can come with a dehydrated face and beard. Includes a dropper to ensure clients get consistent application each and every time. 

Features: Grapeseed Oil, Coconut Oil & Jojoba Oil.

Sold in the following quantities:
10, 20, 30, 40, 50, 75, 100, 150, 200 units

Natural Ingredients to Protect & Hydrate Your Client's Beard & Skin

Our signature Sandalwood Beard Oil blend is cold pressed and contains natural ingredients such as Grape Seed Oil, Coconut Oil, Jojoba Oil & Argan Oil. Our commitment to using high quality ingredients ensures your client's skin and beard get the vitamins, minerals, antioxidants and essential fatty acids they need to keep looking and feeling healthy.

If your client's beard is dry, their skin is just as likely to be dry and itchy. By using a beard oil regularly, they'll be able to ditch the itch - as the oil wicks down their beard and penetrates their hair follicles - keeping their skin and beard well hydrated.

The Subtle Scent of Sandalwood

Not only did we want to provide a beard oil that works great, we wanted to provide your customers with beard oil that left them smelling even better. As they go about their day, the Sandalwood scent of their beard will waft throughout the air, leaving behind the subtle scent of success everywhere they go.

Bottled in Small Batches To Ensure Freshness

Proudly crafted in small batches to ensure each and every beard oil receives the care and attention to detail your client's beard deserves. Our small batch approach means your customers receive a premium beard oil made with fresh ingredients to properly hydrate and protect their beard and face. Bottled in an amber glass bottle to ensure the integrity of the oil blend is not compromised by light.

  • DIRECTIONS FOR BARBERS: For best results, apply to your clients' beard after a trim and rinse, while their beard is still slightly damp and easy to manage. Place onto your fingertips and rub hands together vigorously. Work the oil into your client's beard, beginning at the roots then out to the ends of the hair.
  • AT-HOME DIRECTIONS FOR YOUR CUSTOMERS: For best results, apply to your beard after a shower, while your beard is still slightly damp and easy to manage. Place onto your fingertips and rub hands together vigorously. Work the oil into your beard, beginning at the roots then out to the ends of the hair.

      • Volume: 30 ml
      • Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil, CocosNucifera (Coconut) Oil, Argania Spinosa (Argan) Kernal Oil, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, d-Alpha Tocopherol, Fragrance, Rosmarinus Officinalis (Rosemary) Leaf Extract, Santalum Album (Sandalwood) Oil.
